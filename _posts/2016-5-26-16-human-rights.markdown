### Information
* The Press Freedom index rates North Korea 97/100, with 100 being the worst.
* The Press Status is officially noted as 'Not Free'.
* North Korea's freedom of information and opinion gained attention after the regime threatened war on the United States over a comedy called the Interview, depicting an attempt to assassinate Kim Jong Un.
* North Korea's citizens secretly tune into foreign radios and TV stations, to get uncensored information.
